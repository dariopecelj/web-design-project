About Us

Meet Our Chef: Chef Michael Smith

Chef Michael Smith is the culinary genius behind the extraordinary flavors at Smoke & Sear Steakhouse. With over two decades of experience in the culinary world, Chef Michael has mastered the art of grilling and infusing his steaks with unparalleled taste.

Our Tradition

Established in 2003, Smoke & Sear Steakhouse has been a beacon of culinary excellence in our community for over two decades. Our tradition is rooted in the belief that great food brings people together, and we strive to create memorable dining experiences for each and every one of our customers.

At Smoke & Sear, we uphold the timeless traditions of steakhouse dining while infusing our menu with modern twists and innovations. From our classic cuts to our inventive appetizers and sides, every dish reflects our commitment to quality and creativity.

Our Customers

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a meal with family and friends, or simply treating yourself to a night out, we are dedicated to providing impeccable service and culinary excellence.

We cherish the relationships we've built with our loyal patrons over the years and welcome newcomers to experience the warmth of Smoke & Sear Steakhouse where everyone feels like they are at home.
